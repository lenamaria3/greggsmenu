Why is Greggs a UK Favourite?

Founded in 1939 by John Gregg in Newcastle, Greggs has grown from a single bakery into one of the UK’s most loved food chains. It’s not just the mouthwatering food that makes Greggs special; it’s also their commitment to affordability, quality, and customer convenience. Today, Greggs boasts thousands of outlets across the UK, serving millions of satisfied customers every day.

Here’s why people love Greggs:

  • Freshly prepared meals every day.
  • Affordable prices that fit every budget.
  • Wide range of menu options to cater to all tastes.
  • Quick service for people on the go.
  • Friendly atmosphere with a local bakery feel.
